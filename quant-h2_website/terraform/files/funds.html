Our Fund Interests

Nuclear

We keep a keen eye on companies advancing safe and efficient nuclear energy production. Our models analyze policy changes, technological breakthroughs, and sentiment in the industry to identify potential long-term growth opportunities.

AI/ML

The backbone of quant-h2’s methodology, AI and ML are also areas we invest in. We look for companies pushing the boundaries of machine learning applications, automation, and cognitive computing, ensuring we remain on the forefront of innovation.

Tech

From hardware to software, data centers to cloud platforms, we scout opportunities in emerging technologies that will shape the future of finance, communication, and business operations.

Energy (Batteries)

As the world moves towards renewable energy, battery technology stands as a crucial pillar. We follow trends in battery efficiency, cost reductions, and manufacturing scalability to uncover high-potential investment avenues.

Quantum

Quantum computing holds transformative potential for data analysis, encryption, and complex problem-solving. quant-h2 is poised to back companies that bring us closer to commercially viable quantum solutions.

Space

From satellite networks to asteroid mining, the space sector represents the next frontier. We monitor sentiments around aerospace, launch capabilities, and space-based services to pinpoint opportunities in a sector that’s set to redefine our understanding of commerce and exploration.
